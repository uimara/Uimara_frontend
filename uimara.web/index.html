Uimara translates price volatility into supply volatility using
a rebase mechanism

Imara Token (IMT) is stabe at a price of 1IMT = $0.50
while supply changes proportionately across user wallets.
With IMT, prices of goods and services are ensured to be stable
and more predictable.
Token supply is non-diluent as amount in wallets is percentage that
remains the same with changes in demand and suppy.

When Price < $0.50, wallet balance decrease proportionately

When Price > $0.50, wallet balance increase proportionately
